WHO WE ARE

About Project 100

Project100 is a social impact initiative launched to empower underprivileged youth and underserved communities through targeted interventions. Rooted in our mission to unlock potential and build capacity, we combine hands-on mentorship, structured training, and community-based support to drive sustainable change.

Initially established as a CSR initiative of Dine & Pack Ltd, Project100 aims to address the critical needs of the less privileged through Community Interventions, comprehensive training and support programs.

Since Inception, we have directly and indirectly impacted over 2,000 individuals from underserved communities, helping them to live better lives, unlock potential and launch micro-businesses.

OUR VISION

To create a world where every disadvantaged individual has the opportunity to thrive

Empowering them with skills and support needed to overcome barriers and drive positive change in their communities.

OUR MISSION

Empowering the underprivileged through targeted interventions

Our mission is to unlock their full potential, provide essential skills, and enable them to make meaningful contributions to their immediate societies and the world at large.

OUR FOUNDER

Shetu Gamaliel

Dynamic culinary entrepreneur, educator, and impact-driven changemaker

Shetu Gamaliel is a dynamic culinary entrepreneur, educator, and impact-driven changemaker based in Abuja, Nigeria. With a deep passion for food, community empowerment, and youth development, she has built a multifaceted brand that reflects excellence, resilience, and compassion.

As the founder of Dine and Pack Culinary School and Restaurant, Shetu Gamaliel has trained and mentored hundreds of aspiring chefs and food entrepreneurs. Her school is known for combining practical culinary instruction with real-world business guidance, producing graduates who are confident in both the kitchen and the marketplace.

A bold campaign aimed at providing 100,000+ quality school shoes to children in underserved public schools across Nigeria

Restoring dignity, promoting school attendance, and protecting young feet. We've secured a strategic production partnership with Zujin Projects Ltd (Bata Nigeria) — a trusted, made-in-Nigeria footwear brand — to produce and deliver these shoes at highly discounted rates.

Through this initiative, we are mobilizing sponsors, development partners, and high-net-worth individuals to fund the cost of each pair and extend impact to thousands of children, one step at a time.

Learn More Donate

Why School Shoes Matter

Dignity & Confidence - Proper footwear boosts self-esteem and sense of belonging

Improved Attendance - Children with proper footwear are more likely to attend school regularly
Health Protection - Shoes prevent injuries and parasitic infections from barefoot exposure
