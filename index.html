About Us

Who We Are

Project100 is an international non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged youth through education, skills training, and community development initiatives.

Founded in Nigeria as a CSR initiative of Dine & Pack Ltd, we have grown into a global movement impacting thousands of lives across multiple countries. Our holistic approach combines practical skills training with mentorship and entrepreneurship support to create sustainable change.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged youth through targeted interventions that unlock their full potential, provide essential skills, and enable them to make meaningful contributions to their communities and the world.

Our Vision

A world where every disadvantaged individual has equal opportunity to thrive through access to quality education, skills development, and the support needed to overcome systemic barriers.

Our Founder

Shetu Gamaliel

Dynamic culinary entrepreneur, educator, and impact-driven changemaker

Shetu Gamaliel is a visionary leader and social entrepreneur based in Abuja, Nigeria, whose multifaceted career bridges culinary excellence and youth empowerment.

With a deep passion for food, community development, and education, she has built an inspiring legacy that combines business acumen with compassionate social impact. Her work embodies the belief that sustainable change comes from equipping individuals with both skills and opportunities.

As the founder of Dine and Pack Culinary School and Restaurant, Shetu has trained and mentored hundreds of aspiring chefs and food entrepreneurs. Her institution is renowned for its holistic approach, combining:

  • World-class culinary instruction
  • Practical business training
  • Entrepreneurial mentorship
  • Personal development coaching

This comprehensive model produces graduates who excel both in culinary arts and business management, creating ripple effects throughout their communities.
