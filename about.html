OUR FOUNDER

Shetu Gamaliel

Dynamic culinary entrepreneur, educator, and impact-driven changemaker

Shetu Gamaliel is a dynamic culinary entrepreneur, educator, and impact-driven changemaker based in Abuja, Nigeria. With a deep passion for food, community empowerment, and youth development, she has built a multifaceted brand that reflects excellence, resilience, and compassion.

As the founder of Dine and Pack Culinary School and Restaurant, Shetu Gamaliel has trained and mentored hundreds of aspiring chefs and food entrepreneurs. Her school is known for combining practical culinary instruction with real-world business guidance, producing graduates who are confident in both the kitchen and the marketplace.

Dine and Pack is more than a schoolâ€”it is a launchpad for food-focused dreams. Since its inception, the school has graduated over 300 students, offering intensive short courses in African and continental cuisine, pastries, mixology, and food business management. Shetu Gamaliel has also hosted multiple food exhibitions and pop-up events to showcase her students' talents.

Shetu Gamaliel launched the Dine and Pack Inter-School Cooking Competition, a culinary contest designed to spark interest in food entrepreneurship among teenagers. The competition brought together students from various schools to showcase their culinary talents in a friendly but professional environment.

Shetu Gamaliel is the visionary behind Project100, a social impact initiative committed to empowering underprivileged youth and underserved communities through targeted interventions. Project100 was born out of a deep desire to give back meaningfully to society.

Key highlights include multiple outreach programs at the Zhidu community and internally displaced persons (IDP) camps, where participants benefited from both skill-building programs and critical relief items. Project100 has also conducted community orientations to promote self-reliance, financial literacy, and mental wellness among youth and women.

In her broader leadership capacity, she currently serves as the President of AWCNext (Africa Women Conference Next), a platform that amplifies the voices, ideas, and innovations of African women across sectors.

Shetu Gamaliel's consistent efforts in education and social impact recently earned her the prestigious role of United Nations Girls and Women Speak Ambassador. This appointment recognizes her voice and leadership in championing gender equity and youth empowerment.
