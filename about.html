Our Story

About Project 100

A social impact initiative dedicated to empowering underprivileged youth and underserved communities through targeted interventions, training, and capacity-building programs.

Founded as a CSR initiative of Dine & Pack Ltd, Project 100 has grown from a local effort to address youth unemployment into a comprehensive empowerment program reaching thousands across Nigeria and beyond.

100% Impact

Our name reflects our commitment to creating 100% impact - transforming lives completely through holistic interventions that address education, skills development, mentorship, and economic empowerment.

Empowering Youth

Project 100 supports youth with mentorship and training, creating sustainable pathways out of poverty and into leadership.

From Local to Global

Our journey began in Abuja and now spans several states and international partnerships, aiming to impact 10,000 youth by 2025.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to transforming lives and communities

Our Mission

"Empowering the underprivileged through targeted interventions, training, mentorship, and capacity-building programs."

Our mission is to unlock their full potential, provide essential skills, and enable them to make meaningful contributions to their immediate societies and the world at large. We achieve this through:

  • Practical vocational training programs
  • Comprehensive educational support
  • Mentorship and personal development
  • Entrepreneurship and business support
  • Community-based interventions

Our Vision

"To create a world where every disadvantaged individual has the opportunity to thrive."

We envision empowered communities where youth have the skills and support needed to overcome barriers and drive positive change. Our vision guides us to:

  • Break cycles of poverty through education and skills
  • Create pathways to economic independence
  • Foster leadership and social responsibility
  • Build sustainable community development
  • Promote equality of opportunity for all

"True empowerment comes not from handouts, but from equipping individuals with the tools to transform their own lives and communities."

- Shetu Gamaliel, Founder

Our Founder

Shetu Gamaliel

Dynamic culinary entrepreneur, educator, and impact-driven changemaker

Shetu Gamaliel is a visionary leader and social entrepreneur based in Abuja, Nigeria, whose multifaceted career bridges culinary excellence and youth empowerment.

With a deep passion for food, community development, and education, she has built an inspiring legacy that combines business acumen with compassionate social impact. Her work embodies the belief that sustainable change comes from equipping individuals with both skills and opportunities.

As the founder of Dine and Pack Culinary School and Restaurant, Shetu has trained and mentored hundreds of aspiring chefs and food entrepreneurs. Her institution is renowned for its holistic approach, combining world-class culinary instruction with practical business training and personal development.

Culinary Achievements
  • Established premier culinary institute
  • Trained 500+ professional chefs
  • Pioneer in Nigerian culinary education
Leadership & Recognition
