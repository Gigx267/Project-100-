Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Project 100 and our initiatives.

We welcome volunteers in various capacities. You can fill out our volunteer application form indicating your skills and availability. Our team will contact you with opportunities that match your profile.

You can make a one-time or recurring donation through our donation page. We also welcome corporate sponsorships and in-kind donations. For significant contributions, please contact us directly to discuss how we can best apply your support.

We collaborate with organizations in various ways including program implementation, employee volunteering, CSR initiatives, and cause-related marketing. Please email us at partnerships@project100.org with details about your organization and proposed collaboration.

Application forms for our various training programs are available on our training application page. Please complete the form for the program you're interested in and our team will contact eligible candidates.

We provide regular impact reports to our donors through email newsletters and our annual report. Major donors receive personalized updates about the specific programs they support. You can also follow our social media channels for real-time updates on our activities.
