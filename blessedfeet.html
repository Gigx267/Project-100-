About BlessedFeet

BlessedFeet is a targeted initiative to provide quality school shoes to children in underserved public schools across Nigeria. Many children attend school barefoot or with inadequate footwear, which affects their health, comfort, and school attendance.

We partner with Zujin Projects Ltd (Bata Nigeria) to produce durable, comfortable school shoes at discounted rates for distribution to children in need.

0 Pairs Distributed
25 Schools Reached

Why School Shoes Matter

  • Dignity & Confidence: Proper footwear boosts self-esteem and sense of belonging
  • Improved Attendance: Children with proper footwear are more likely to attend school regularly
  • Health Protection: Shoes prevent injuries and parasitic infections from barefoot exposure
  • Academic Performance: Comfortable students can focus better on their studies
  • Long-term Impact: Education is the most powerful tool to break the cycle of poverty

How The Initiative Works

Our systematic approach ensures we reach the children who need shoes the most

1

Identify Schools

We partner with local authorities to identify underserved public schools with the greatest need

2

Foot Measurement

Volunteers visit schools to measure children's feet for proper sizing

3

Shoe Production

Our manufacturing partner produces high-quality, durable school shoes

4

Distribution Day

We organize school visits to distribute shoes directly to students
